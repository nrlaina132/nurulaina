SCHOOL AND UNIVERSITY YEAR

         In 2007 to 2012, I receive my education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Kompleks KLIA (SKKKLIA). It takes 4 minutes to arrive to my school. Next, I furthered my studies from the year of 2013 until 2017 at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Kompleks KLIA (SMKKKLIA) where it is next to my primary school.

         I also did not miss out to get involved in various activities in school. I involved in activities such as sports, science projects, marching and more. How I missed my school days and friends. I have bittersweet memories with my classmates and those memories I will never forget. It made a deep impression because that is when we were able to spend time together every day at school.


         As time passes, in 2018 I got an offer to further my studies at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kampus Rembau, Cawangan Negeri Sembilan. There are a lot of memories and experiences I will never forget. Two and a half years is a short time but there are a lot of things I have learned and I became more mature in making a decision.

         I had the opportunity to make new friends with student from different faculties and other states. In addition, I was able to stay in college for 4 semesters, Alhamdulillah but in the fourth semester of 2020, we all had to go home in the third week of the semester due to the Covid-19 pandemic that hit all country.
